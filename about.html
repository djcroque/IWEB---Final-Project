Our History: Setting the Standard in Pampanga

GalaCy Plaza and Events Center began operations in 2018 in Floridablanca, Pampanga. Founded by a married couple, the venue was designed to host diverse events, quickly gaining a reputation for its flexible solutions and top-quality service. The founders consistently reinvested profits to ensure continuous venue improvements.

With a strong focus on client relationships, GalaCy Plaza delivered personalized and memorable events. By 2024, this growing success led to the opening of Galleria in Guagua, Pampanga, a sister venue that expanded the brand’s market reach and provided additional event space options.

The GalaCy Difference: Setting Industry Benchmarks

Event Excellence

Today, GalaCy Plaza and Events Center is recognized as Pampanga’s leading event rental venue. With continued growth and success, the founders remain dedicated to expanding event rental services across Central Luzon. We prioritize flexibility, quality, and innovation in every client partnership.

Future Focus

Looking ahead, the company aims to set industry benchmarks by introducing versatile event solutions for corporate functions, business meetings, and personal celebrations. With a commitment to excellence and strong customer connections, GalaCy Plaza and Events Center continues to create unique and unforgettable event experiences for every client.
