About GalaCy Plaza

Established in 2018 in Floridablanca, Pampanga, GalaCy Plaza is a premier multi-use complex designed for enterprise and excitement. We are a community hub where business, leisure, and large-scale events converge.

  • Prime location for maximum visibility
  • Supportive community for new entrepreneurs
  • Unique blend of commercial and entertainment offerings
  • Modern, flexible event spaces
